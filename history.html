Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Johor (Rumah Barkat) is a childcare house that are located at Jalan Bukit Chagar, Johor Bahru. Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Johor (Rumah Barkat) was established on 1 January 1979 at “Bilik Gerakan”, District office, Jalan Duke Johor Bahru. On 30 October 1979, the Johor state government lend an officer's residential house that is located at No.118, Jalan Bukit Chagar, Johor Bahru to this organization and it was named as “Rumah Barkat”. On 26 April 1980, “Rumah Barkat” building was renovated and place only 15 children that met the specified requirements. The requirements include the child is an orphan, not older than 17 years old, the application is made through the Department of Social Welfare, has a confirmation report from doctors and the guardian income does not exceed RM 900.00 per month. From year to year, the number of children that live in “Rumah Barkat” has increased from 15 children to 120 children. All the children that live in “Rumah Barkat” are fully funded until they finish their study at institutions of higher learning. On 15 May 1983, “Rumah Barkat” expanded its building by adding two building blocks for the boys and the girls, built a dining hall and also built six buildings for the staff house. All of these renovations can be done with the support and donation from the philanthropic members. The house that previously lent to this organization was fully given to this organization in December 1999. With all the support received by Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-anak Yatim Johor, “Rumah Barkat” has been renovated and now has two dormitory blocks, hall, library, treatment room, guest room and also surau.
